## Custom Post Types (CPT)

| CPT Slug | Label | Archive Slug | Supports | Icon |
|----------|-------|-------------|----------|------|
| city | Cities | /cities/ | title, editor, thumbnail, excerpt | dashicons-location-alt |
| faq | FAQs | /faqs/ | title, editor, thumbnail, excerpt | dashicons-editor-help |
| service | Services | /services/ | title, editor, thumbnail, excerpt | dashicons-hammer |

All CPTs are: public, queryable, show in REST, show in nav menus, non-hierarchical.

## Taxonomies

| Taxonomy Slug | Label | Type | Attached To |
|--------------|-------|------|-------------|
| faq_category | FAQ Categories | Hierarchical | faq |
| service_type | Service Types | Hierarchical | service |
| location | Locations | Hierarchical | city, service |

All taxonomies are: public, queryable, show in REST, show in nav menus.

---

## Data Structure Relationships

- **City** CPT → uses `location` taxonomy → has ACF group: City Details
- **Service** CPT → uses `service_type` taxonomy + `location` taxonomy → has ACF group: Service Details
- **FAQ** CPT → uses `faq_category` taxonomy → has ACF group: FAQ Details → has relationship field to City
- **Pages** → has ACF group: Page Details
- **Site Settings** → ACF options page → has ACF group: Site Settings
